About the website

HACK-X is a platform designed to help students and developers easily discover and register for hackathons. Our website provides a simple and convenient way to explore upcoming hackathon events, learn about their details, and register without any complicated process. The goal of this platform is to bring together individuals who are passionate about technology, innovation, and problem solving, and give them a space where they can participate in exciting hackathons and showcase their skills.
